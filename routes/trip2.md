Day 1 — Fri 18 Apr
Warsaw → Brno
~5h drive · ~560 km
Evening stroll
Motorway south via Ostrava. Arrive late afternoon. Brno's old town is compact and lively — walk up Špilberk Castle hill for a sunset view. Supercharger at Brno Globus.
Sleep: Brno
Day 2 — Sat 19 Apr
Brno – Moravian Karst cycling
Cycling ~35–50 km
Optional cave walk
The Moravian Karst is a 25-min drive north. A paved trail follows the Punkva valley — flat to gently rolling, beautiful limestone gorges. Rent bikes in Blansko. The Punkva Caves (boat ride underground) are worth a detour.
Sleep: Brno
Day 3 — Sun 20 Apr
Brno → Vienna → Graz
~3h drive · ~310 km total
Vienna stop
Stop in Vienna for 2–3 hours — walk the Ringstrasse, grab coffee. Then continue south on the A2 to Graz. Graz is underrated: Schlossberg hill, beautiful Altstadt, great food scene. Tesla Supercharger at Graz SCS.
Sleep: Graz
Day 4 — Mon 21 Apr
Graz area cycling — Mur cycle path
Cycling ~40–60 km
Schlossberg hike option
The Murradweg (Mur cycle path) runs right through Graz along the river — head south toward Leibnitz through vineyards and rolling hills. Very gentle terrain, April is peak green season here (~16–18°C).
Sleep: Graz
Day 5 — Tue 22 Apr
Graz → Lake Bled, Slovenia
~2.5h drive · ~220 km
Bled afternoon hike
Cross into Slovenia via the Karawanken tunnel. Lake Bled is one of Europe's most beautiful spots — the island church, the castle on a cliff. Arrive early afternoon and hike up to Ojstrica viewpoint (30 min, easy) for the classic panorama. Supercharger in Bled.
Sleep: Lake Bled
Day 6 — Wed 23 Apr
Bled — cycling + Triglav foothills hike
Cycling ~30 km
Vintgar Gorge hike
Morning: cycle around the lake and toward Bohinj valley (e-bikes available locally for the hillier sections). Afternoon: walk Vintgar Gorge — a stunning 1.6 km canyon boardwalk along the Radovna river. April has fewer crowds and waterfalls are at full flow.
Sleep: Lake Bled
Day 7 — Thu 24 Apr
Bled → Ljubljana → Trieste or Venice area
~2.5h drive
Ljubljana old town
Stop in Ljubljana for 2 hours — walk the castle, grab lunch by the river. Then continue toward the Adriatic. Trieste is a beautiful Habsburg port city, or push to the Veneto/Friuli coast. Temperature climbs to 18–20°C here in late April.
Sleep: Trieste or Gorizia
Day 8 — Fri 25 Apr
Day trip to Venice + Adriatic coast cycling
~1.5h to Venice
Coastal cycling
Venice
Drive to Venice for the morning/afternoon (park at Tronchetto, take the vaporetto in). Return late afternoon. Or skip Venice and spend the day cycling the Parenzana trail — a converted railway through Slovenian/Italian Karst with sea views. A public holiday in Italy (Liberation Day) but Venice is always crowded regardless.
Sleep: Trieste / coast
Day 9 — Sat 26 Apr
Return leg begins — head north
~4h drive
Comfortable drive back toward Vienna or Bratislava. Either continue to Warsaw overnight (long day, ~10h total) or split over two days with a final night in Vienna or Bratislava. Bratislava is only 1h from Vienna and has a Supercharger — nice compact city for a last evening.
Sleep: Bratislava or Vienna
